About the Sound Design

In a futuristic neon-lit city, every turn is a battle against time, and every sound tells a story. For Neon Rush, I crafted a full spectrum of sounds capturing the adrenaline and rhythm of high-speed arcade racing — from roaring turbo engines to synthetic UI elements and the atmospheric hum of a city alive at night.

Dynamic audio layers react to acceleration, drifts, and collisions, creating a powerful sense of motion and energy. You can feel the rhythm of the asphalt, the screech of tires, and the echo of music bouncing off glass skyscrapers. The goal was to merge retro-futuristic aesthetics with modern cinematic sound design, so that speed could be heard as vividly as it’s seen.
